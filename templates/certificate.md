# Certificate Template

Use this text for simple participant certificates. Keep it typographic. Do not turn it into a fake credential.

```text
AI-Native Citizens: [City]

Certificate of Participation

This certifies that

[Participant Name]

participated in the Civic AI Sprint held on [dates] in [city].

They contributed to a public-interest AI prototype and published the work through the AI-Native Citizens build-log standard.

Project:
[Project Name]

Host:
[Host Organization]

Local Lead:
[Name]

Date:
[Date]

Repository:
https://github.com/baditaflorin/ai-native-citizens
```
