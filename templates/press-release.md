## AI-Native Citizens Comes to [City] With a Civic AI Build Sprint

[City], [Date] - AI-Native Citizens: [City] will bring residents, students, civic groups, journalists, designers, developers, mentors, and public partners together to build working AI prototypes for local public problems.

The sprint runs from [date] to [date] at [venue]. Teams will work on challenges such as public-service navigation, budget transparency, council decision explainers, procurement watching, school rights, NGO admin, local journalism, and PDF-to-open-data conversion.

"People do not learn AI from keynotes. They learn AI by building something useful for their community," said [local lead]. "This sprint is about public problems, working prototypes, and visible build logs."

Every team will publish an AI build log documenting the prompts used, what AI generated well, what humans corrected, what works, and what still does not work. Finalists will demo publicly on [demo day date].

AI-Native Citizens is an open movement for local civic AI build sprints. The kit includes the organizer playbook, challenge bank, workflow cards, judging rubric, mentor manual, demo-day kit, templates, and post-event continuation guidance.
