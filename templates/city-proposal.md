# City Proposal

## Proposal

Bring AI-Native Citizens to [City] as a 48 to 72 hour civic AI sprint focused on real public problems.

## Host

Preferred host: university, neutral civic NGO, library, or other public-interest venue.

Municipality role: partner-of-record, challenge source, data/source contact, demo-day participant, and continuation partner.

## Why [City]

Write 3 to 5 sentences about local public problems, civic groups, universities, tech capacity, youth participation, and administrative pain points.

## Sprint Outputs

- 5 or more local civic challenges.
- 10 to 20 teams.
- Working prototypes.
- Mandatory build logs.
- Public demo.
- Project index.
- 30-day follow-up report.

## Proposed Challenge Areas

- Public services.
- Budget transparency.
- Council decisions.
- Procurement.
- Education rights.
- NGO admin.
- Local journalism.
- Open data.

## Required Local Support

- Venue.
- Wi-Fi and power.
- Local lead and ops support.
- Challenge contacts.
- Mentors.
- Demo-day guests.
- Small continuation budget if possible.

## Boundaries

Projects belong to teams. No prototype is promised as production software. Any continuation requires explicit consent, credit, and a micro-grant, contract, or other written support.
