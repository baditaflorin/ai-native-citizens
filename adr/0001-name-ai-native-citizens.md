# ADR-0001: Name the Movement AI-Native Citizens

## Context

The project needs a name that clearly identifies a civic movement, not a vendor, tool, or one-off event. The phrase must connect to the lineage of digital natives and cloud-native practice while avoiding the mistaken reading that "AI citizens" means artificial agents with civic rights.

## Decision

The movement is named **AI-Native Citizens**. The hyphen is mandatory in the full brand because "AI-Native" is the compound modifier. The short form is **AI Natives** only after the full name is established. Local chapters use **AI Natives of [City]**. Events use **AI-Native Citizens: [City] ([dates])**.

## Consequences

The name is direct, global, and easy to adapt locally. It can hold identity, method, kit, and event formats without becoming a product name. Writers must protect the hyphen on first reference. Public copy must not shorten the name to "AI Citizens" because that changes the meaning.

## Alternatives Considered

"Civic AI Sprints" described the method but not the identity. "Public AI Builders" was clear but lacked lineage. "AI Citizens" was rejected because it suggests a sci-fi rights movement rather than humans building with AI.
